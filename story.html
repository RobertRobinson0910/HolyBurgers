The Beginning

Holy Burgers has a truly unique beginning, starting back in 2023 as a creative project. It all took place within the Roblox game Obby Creator (created by mario118118); a space where we built our first conceptual restaurant. This project was initially a joke created purely for fun (and we had a different name then!). While our first build, V1, was simple, it was packed with memorable features that our early fans loved, including a chaotic flooding system and a secret safe you could steal from the small kitchen. This inventive spirit helped us reach over 100 likes and established the chaotic, unforgettable era that is the foundation of Holy Burgers today. As our vision for this project grew, we changed our name to Holy Burgers, bringing that original creativity and spirit out of the game and into our mission.

The Remake

Just six months after the original launch, we committed to refining the Holy Burgers concept by creating a full remaster (still within Obby Creator). This new era brought a better-designed fast-food building and a refreshed Holy Burgers logo. Most importantly, this version introduced a major innovation: the HB Kiosk system. This custom-built restaurant POS system revolutionized how our fans interacted with the game, making the experience more engaging, dynamic, and realistic.

The last weeks in Obby Creator

Just one year after the original concept, the third major version of Holy Burgers arrived. Although V3 was our shortest-running version, it was a pivotal chapter in our evolution. It introduced significant technical improvements, including realistic lighting and fully remastered systems, all built within Obby Creator. V3 became the largest fast-food location we had ever built and allowed us to manage our highest number of dedicated staff members to date, proving the incredible potential and community interest in the Holy Burgers brand.

The transition from Obby Creator to Roblox Studio

Recognizing the limitations of Obby Creator, the Holy Burgers team decided to make a major leap: moving development entirely to Roblox Studio. This allowed us to build a dedicated, feature-rich game designed specifically for the Holy Burgers experience. To facilitate this transition, we rebranded our existing supermarket game, Freemart, transforming it into a fast-food environment to continue the legacy of HB. This ambitious project is constantly introducing new features and innovations. We are excited to announce that this new, fully-featured Holy Burgers experience has officially reached the public beta stage! Come and explore the future of HB.

The Future

The HB team is currently focused on wrapping up the beta phase by finishing and polishing all in-game mechanics, and preparing for the release of our official training center.
